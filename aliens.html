Do aliens exist?

Origin of the 'alien idea'

The idea of aliens first came up in 200AD, in eastern Turkey.The Lucian of Samosta had written a book called 'Vera Historia/True story.'
The story talked about aliens on the moon, which we now know is false.
After the Lucian, many other people started adopting the idea, and now, the term 'aliens' has spread world-wide.

How we are looking for alien life

The Search for Extra-Terrestrial Intelligence ,or SETI,has been looking for aliens since 1980.
It uses advanced technology to send and recieve signals, and is headquartered at California,USA.

Commonly-known aliens

Grey aliens

The grey aliens are also known as Zeta Reticulans, Rosswell grays, or Grays.
They have 'small bodies with smooth grey-colored skin, enlarged hairless heads, and large black eyes'.

Aliens under the ice

Recently, scientists in Antartica discovered a so-called 'alien base' under a cave in Antartica.
NASA has built a device called 'BRUIE', which can explore underneath the ice and test for alien signals.
